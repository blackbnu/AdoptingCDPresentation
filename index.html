Continuous Delivery

O que é?

Making sure your software is always production ready throughout its entire lifecycle – that any build could potentially be released to users at the touch of a button using a fully automated process in a matter of seconds or minutes.

http://continuousdelivery.com/2010/08/continuous-delivery-vs-continuous-deployment/

Por que adotar?

  • Reduzir custo, tempo e risco entregando mudanças incrementais ao usuário
  • Entregar a funcionalidade certa através do rápido feedback sobre hipóteses

Princípios

  • Create a repeatable, reliable process for releasing software
  • Automate almost everything (build, deploy, test, release / use humans for high value stuff)
  • Keep everything in version control
  • If it hurts, do it more often, and bring the pain forward
  • Build quality in (no inspection, process insures quality)
  • Done means released
  • Everybody is responsible for delivery
  • Continuous improvement
